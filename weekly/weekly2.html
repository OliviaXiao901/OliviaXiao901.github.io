How was the internet shaped by Objectivism? What is Californian Ideology

According to the philosophy of Ayn Rand, "the concept of man as a heroic being, with his happiness as the moral purpose of his life, with productive achievement as his noblest activity, and reason as his only absolute." She advocates that the government should set people free and give less control to the market. This idea can be presented through the internet.

One thing that impresses me is how this Objectivism idea connects people without controlling their behaviors. Just as the video shows, when people are playing video games together, though they are free to act, they all have the same goal which is to win the game. That is why their behaviors are united with little disagreements. This idea reflects what the internet world looks like in the modern world. Though we do not have a clear rule in the internet world, since we share the same morality, the rule then is gradually being formed.

Californian ideology refers to a kind of freedom which let individuals do their things and choose on their own. This ideology seems to be an extension of Ayn Rand philosophy and computer utopia. Liberating individuals also has a political meaning which means less restriction to personal freedom. But the ideology is based on a conform morality shared by people, which means that less control may still lead to social problems if the moral line is blurred.
